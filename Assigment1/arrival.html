About Arrival:

Arrival was founded in 2015 by Denis Sverdlov.
Its a global company with headquarters in Kensington,
London and Charlotte, North Carolina.
As stated on their website "we are reinventing both the design
and production of electric vehicles for end to end sustainability".

Arrivals EV Selection

The Arrival Car

About the Arrival Car

This car is mainly designed to be ideal for ride sharing.
It has been designed in partnership with Uber.

The Cars key features inclue:
  • Extra leg room for passengers
  • Increased visability
  • A range of 180km per charge

The Arrival Bus

About the Arrival Bus

This bus is designed to be inclusive to all passengers

The Buses key features inclue:
